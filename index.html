COME & DISCOVER

It's more fun in the Philippines... beautiful beaches, amazing wild life, stunning landscapes, ocean adventures, cultural and culinary delights, the warmth and hospitality of the Filipino people.

10 REASONS WHY IT'S MORE FUN IN THE PHILIPPINES

1. WHALE SHARKS

See and snorkel near whale sharks in Donsol in Sorsogon.

2. PRISTINE AND UNSPOILED BEACHES

Take a dip in the crystal-clear waters of one of many Philippine beaches.

3. HIKING

Appreciate nature by hiking along the trails of Mount Makiling.

4. KAWASAN FALLS

Go cliff diving, canyoneering or swimming at Kawasan Falls.

5. WHITEWATER RAFTING

Go white-water rafting in the Cagayan-de Oro River with its electrifying rapids.

6. THE FESTIVITIES

Experience the festivals in the Philippines - full of parades and street dances in honour of their culture.

7. ISLAND HOPPING

The Philippines has 7,461 islands waiting to be explored!.

8. EXPLORE BY CAR

Drive the scenic coastal highways of Leyte.

9. TUBBATAHA REEFS

Discover one of the world's best dive spots in Palawan.

10. RICE TERRACES

Walk through the rice terraces in the province of Ifugao.
