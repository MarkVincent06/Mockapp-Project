Travel Inspirations

When we dream of traveling, we imagine all the beautiful places we want to go to. Discover what breathtaking sceneries and memorable activities you can enjoy in the Philippines.

#FUN AWAITS YOU IN THE PHILIPPINES

The Philippines is home to world-renowned natural wonders like an underground river and rice terraces, incredible diving spots rich in biodiversity, colorful public transportation, unique cuisine, vibrant festivals that showcase its colorful culture, and friendly locals regarded as some of the happiest in the world.

BEACHES AND ISLANDS

Made up of 7,641 islands, the Philippines is one of the most biodiverse places on the planet. If you're looking for the most pristine beaches and postcard-worthy views, the Philippines has a ton of them!

ACTION ADVENTURE

Satisfy your thirst for adventure whether it be on land or in the water. There are many adrenaline-pumping adventures that await you in the Philippines!

DIVE DEEPER

Jump in and discover dramatic biodiversity, where unique wreck dives, rainbow-coloured reefs and a richly varied marine life all await you.

CULTURAL DELIGHTS

Immerse yourself in the rich culture and history of the Philippines. From museums and architecture to cultural festivities and live music, there are many ways to get under the skin of Filipino culture.

NATURAL WONDERS

Go beyond Philippines beaches and surround yourself with breath-taking landscapes in the wilderness. Wander through rice terraces, encounter local wildlife and chase waterfalls.

ROMANTIC GETAWAYS
